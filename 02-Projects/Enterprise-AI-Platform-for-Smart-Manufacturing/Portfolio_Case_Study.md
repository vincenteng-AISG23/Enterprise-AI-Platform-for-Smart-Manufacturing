# AI-Driven Smart Manufacturing Control Tower

## 1. Executive Summary

This project demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can be applied at an enterprise level to unify key manufacturing functions into a single decision-support platform.

The solution integrates three core domains:
- Predictive Maintenance (Reliability)
- Defect Detection (Quality)
- Demand Forecasting (Planning)

Instead of isolated AI models, this platform provides a **unified control tower** that translates data into operational and financial decisions.

---

## 2. Business Problem

Manufacturing environments typically operate in silos:

- Maintenance teams focus on machine uptime
- Quality teams focus on defect detection
- Planning teams focus on demand forecasting

These functions are rarely connected, resulting in:
- Unplanned downtime
- High defect rates
- Poor production planning
- Lack of visibility for leadership

Most AI implementations fail because they optimize individual processes rather than the entire operation.

---

## 3. Solution Overview

To address this, I designed and built an **AI-Driven Smart Manufacturing Control Tower**.

The platform integrates:

### Predictive Maintenance
- Machine failure prediction using ML
- Risk classification (High / Medium / Low)
- Estimated financial impact (downtime cost)

### Defect Detection
- Quality inspection analytics
- Defect rate tracking
- Confidence scoring for inspection outputs

### Demand Forecasting
- Historical demand trend analysis
- Forecast vs actual comparison
- Forecast accuracy and bias detection

---

## 4. Unified Executive Control Tower

The most critical component is the **Executive Control Layer**.

This layer:
- Aggregates data across all modules
- Translates technical outputs into business KPIs
- Provides financial visibility (risk exposure)
- Generates cross-functional insights

### Example KPIs:
- Total Risk Exposure (SGD)
- High Risk Machines
- Defect Rate
- Forecast Accuracy

---

## 5. Cross-Module Intelligence

Unlike traditional AI projects, this platform connects signals across domains.

### Example Insights:
- High machine failure risk can increase defect rates
- Maintenance instability can disrupt production planning
- Poor forecast accuracy combined with downtime amplifies operational risk

This enables leadership to:
- Prioritize interventions
- Allocate resources effectively
- Make proactive decisions

## 7. Technology Architecture

The platform is built using:

- Python
- Streamlit (UI / dashboard)
- Scikit-learn (ML models)
- Pandas (data processing)
- Matplotlib (visualization)

### Data Flow:

Data Sources (ERP / MES / Sensors / Images)  
↓  
Data Processing & Feature Engineering  
↓  
Machine Learning Models  
↓  
KPI Calculation & Risk Scoring  
↓  
Executive Dashboard (Control Tower)
