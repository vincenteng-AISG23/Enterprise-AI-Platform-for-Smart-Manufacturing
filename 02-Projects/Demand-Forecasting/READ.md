# 📊 AI Demand Forecasting for Smart Supply Chain

## 👨‍💼 Executive Summary
This project demonstrates how AI can predict product demand using historical sales data, pricing, and promotional signals.

By leveraging machine learning, businesses can shift from reactive inventory management to proactive demand planning, reducing stockouts and excess inventory.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
In manufacturing and retail environments, inaccurate demand forecasting leads to:
- Overstocking → higher holding costs
- Stockouts → lost revenue
- Inefficient production planning

---

## 💡 Solution
This project builds a machine learning model to predict product demand based on:

- Historical demand
- Pricing strategy
- Promotional campaigns
- Day-of-week patterns

---

## ⚙️ Technology Stack
- Python
- Pandas (data processing)
- Scikit-learn (machine learning)
- Matplotlib (visualization)

---

## 📈 Results
- MAE: ~5 units
- RMSE: ~6 units

Key drivers of demand:
- Day of week
- Price changes
- Promotions

---

## 🏭 Business Impact
This solution enables:

- Better inventory planning
- Optimized pricing strategies
- Improved production scheduling
- Reduced operational cost
