# MSc-Artificial-Intelligence (Univeristy of Colorado Boulder)
This repository contains coursework, assignments, and projects completed as part of the Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence (MS-AI) program. The program provides an in-depth exploration of the theory, tools, and practical applications of artificial intelligence, enabling learners to specialize in AI and machine learning and engage with technologies at the forefront of innovation.

Program Focus

The MS-AI program provides a focused study of the technologies transforming modern industries. The curriculum develops advanced knowledge in areas such as neural networks, deep learning, generative AI, and reinforcement learning. Through applied coursework and projects, students gain experience designing, training, and deploying intelligent systems used in real-world products and services.

AI Development Lifecycle

The program emphasizes the full lifecycle of AI systems, including:

Building and training machine learning models

Evaluating and improving model performance

Deploying AI systems at scale

Managing AI solutions across real-world applications

In addition to technical expertise, the program also addresses responsible AI practices, including understanding bias, fairness, and the broader societal impact of artificial intelligence.

Purpose of This Repository

This repository documents the learning journey throughout the MS-AI program and demonstrates the practical implementation of AI concepts through experiments, assignments, and applied projects.
